# Pingmart 🛍️

**WhatsApp-powered order management for Nigerian vendors.**

Pingmart lets you run your entire store through WhatsApp — no website, no app, no technical setup required. Customers browse, order, and pay without leaving their chat.

---

## Who It's For

- Food vendors, fashion sellers, and product-based businesses
- Course creators, ebook authors, and digital product sellers
- Any Nigerian vendor who wants to sell directly on WhatsApp

---

## What It Does

- Customers place orders by chatting with your WhatsApp number
- Supports Paystack payments and bank transfers
- Instant order notifications sent directly to your WhatsApp
- Manage and confirm orders from your phone — no dashboard needed
- Handles both physical goods and digital product delivery
