Welcome to Pingmart. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our WhatsApp-based order management service.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Pingmart through WhatsApp, we may collect the following information:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your name (if provided)
  • Your delivery address (for physical product orders)
  • Order details including products selected, quantities, and payment method
  • Payment confirmation information (e.g. payment screenshots for bank transfers)
  • Your preferred language for communication

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Send you order confirmations and delivery updates via WhatsApp
  • Notify vendors of incoming orders
  • Deliver digital products (e.g. ebooks, course files) to you
  • Improve the performance and reliability of our service
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Vendors: Your order details and delivery address are shared with the vendor fulfilling your order.
  • Payment processors: We use Paystack to process payments. Your payment information is handled directly by Paystack under their own privacy policy.
  • Cloud services: We use Cloudinary for secure file storage (e.g. payment proof images, digital files).
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

4. WhatsApp and Meta

Pingmart operates through the WhatsApp Business Cloud API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. By messaging Pingmart on WhatsApp, you also agree to WhatsApp's Terms of Service and Meta's Privacy Policy. We access only the messages you send directly to our business number.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted databases. We apply industry-standard security practices including:

  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data
  • Strict access controls limiting who can view your information
  • No storage of full payment card details

6. Data Retention

We retain your order history and account information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of our service at any time by ceasing to interact with our WhatsApp number

8. Children's Privacy

Pingmart is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
